Contributions to the Registry


During our project, we generated multiple basic and composite parts, and characterized and improved some existing parts. We created Cytolysin A (Y171F) (BBa_K4359001) an attenuated version of Cytolysin A for future teams to safely work with. We added to the characterization of the myc tag (BBa_K823036), and demonstrated that anti myc antibodies cross react with endogenous c-myc present in many mammalian cell lines, making it incompatible for use with such systems.

Bacterial Outer Membrane Vesicles: A Guide


As a team we are very passionate about OMVs and their potential in diverse fields of biomedical research. We performed extensive literature review regar̥ding the composition, biogenesis, bioengineering of OMVs and their applications in vaccines, as adjuvants, diagnostics. We wanted to collate this information and present an OMV handbook that can be used as a reference by iGEM teams and other researchers in the future.
